About me

Hi, I’m Pranav. I believe that the best solutions are found by challenging our existing notions. I am determined to have a positive impact by transforming how people interact with technology.

I currently work as a Product Manager in the supply chain management industry. Building products that positively disrupt processes is my passion. As an Industrial Engineer in college at UC Berkeley, I took multiple courses on UI/UX design, Entrepreneurship and Data Management.

In my free time I shoot and edit cooking videos, paint, do Muay Thai and travel. Read more about my projects below.
